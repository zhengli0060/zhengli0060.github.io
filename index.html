Biography

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Hao Zhang and Prof. Ruxin Wang. I received my M.S. degree from Beijing Technology and Business University (September 2023 - July 2025), advised by Prof. Feng Xie, and was a student member of the Causal Inference Group led by Prof. Zhi Geng.

Research Interests

  • Causal discovery in the presence of latent variables
  • Selecting valid adjustment sets for causal effect estimation
  • Testability of instrumental variables

I am happy to have academic discussions and collaborations. Please feel free to contact me!

Open Source Projects

  • FastDAG2PAG: DAG to PAG/MAG Converter
    A Python implementation for transforming Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs) into Maximal Ancestral Graphs (MAGs) and Partial Ancestral Graphs (PAGs) given specified latent variables and selection bias. This tool implements Theorem 4.2 proposed by Richardson and Spirtes in '' Ancestral Graph Markov Models'' (The Annals of Statistics, 2002), which achieves efficient conversion.
